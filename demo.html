How to read the gallery

Six capabilities on display

Use the filter chips above the gallery to isolate one capability at a time, or browse all 23 rollouts together.

01 — Simple pick

Training and held-out

Four rollouts of "Pick the red cube" — from a training replay to held-out episodes 50 and 100. The model successfully reaches and grasps the cube on every one.

02 — Mid-episode recovery

Resume from any state

Six rollouts that start deep inside a trajectory — the descend, grasp, and lift phases of two held-out episodes. The model continues the task regardless of starting pose.

03 — Pick and place

Container manipulation

A full autoregressive rollout of picking the red cube and placing it in the bowl. Six seconds of imagined video and joint commands from a single initial frame.

04 — Stacking

Two-object sequencing

Stack the red cube on the blue one. The model has to plan a two-step trajectory — approach, grasp, lift, translate, align, release — entirely in imagination.

05 — Novel instructions

Language conditioning

Same three scenes, language instructions the model never saw during training ("blue cube" instead of "red cube"). Tests whether the model actually attends to the language token.

06 — Zero-shot task

Out-of-distribution

Eight samples on so101_box_to_bowl_v2 — a dataset the model was never trained on. Demonstrates raw generalization from the aggregated SO-101 pretraining mix.

DreamZero-SO101

An open world-action model for the low-cost SO-101 teleoperator arm. Fine-tuned from Wan2.1-I2V-14B on aggregated LeRobot SO-101 community datasets.

Released under Apache 2.0 by Vizuara.
