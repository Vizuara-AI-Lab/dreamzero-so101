How to read the gallery

What you are looking at

01 — Single initial frame

No live observations

The only real information the model receives is one frame from the front camera and the robot's starting joint state. Everything else is imagined — the model then chains its own predicted video back as input for the next chunk.

02 — Training vs novel

The prompt barely changes the trajectory

Compare "Pick the red cube" with "Pick the blue cube" on the same initial frame — the motion is almost identical. The model's autoregressive video stream tends to dominate the language signal after the first few chunks.

03 — Three cameras

Front, top, gripper

The model was trained on the three-camera SO-101 rig from whosricky/so101-megamix-v1. All three views are imagined simultaneously from the shared latent and decoded through the Wan VAE in a 2×2 mosaic.

DreamZero-SO101

An open world-action model for the low-cost SO-101 teleoperator arm. Fine-tuned from Wan2.1-I2V-14B on aggregated LeRobot SO-101 community datasets.

Released under Apache 2.0 by Vizuara.
