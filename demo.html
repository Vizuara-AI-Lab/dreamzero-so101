How to read the gallery

Six capabilities on display

01 — Simple pick

Training + held-out

Four rollouts of "Pick the red cube" — from an ep-0 training replay to held-out episodes 50 and 100. Action RMSE under 2.3° across all of them.

02 — Mid-episode phases

Resume from any state

Six rollouts that start mid-trajectory — descend, grasp, and lift phases of episodes 50 and 100. Shows the model does not depend on the canonical starting pose.

03 — Pick & place

Container manipulation

A full DreamGen rollout of picking the red cube and placing it in the bowl. 12 seconds of autoregressive video + action prediction from one initial frame.

04 — Stack

Two-object sequencing

Stack the red cube on the blue one. The model has to plan a two-step trajectory — approach red, lift, descend onto blue, release — entirely in imagination.

05 — Novel prompts

Language robustness

Same three scenes, flipped prompts ("blue cube" vs "red cube"). Tests whether the model is actually language-conditioned or just replaying training motions.

06 — Zero-shot unseen task

Out-of-distribution

Eight single-chunk samples on so101_box_to_bowl_v2 — a dataset the model was never trained on. Best sample at 1.57° RMSE; raw generalization of the SO-101 pretraining mix.

DreamZero-SO101

An open world-action model for the low-cost SO-101 teleoperator arm. Fine-tuned from Wan2.1-I2V-14B on aggregated LeRobot SO-101 community datasets.
